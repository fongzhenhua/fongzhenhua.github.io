为了顺利通过大学英语四级考试，坚持每天写至少一篇范文，在熟练的同时，也可以留下一份资料。

Are people becoming addicted to technology?

2022-11-09

In this digital age, technology seems to have become a part of our lives as we rely on it for work, study and even entertainment. More and more people find it difficult to live without technology , especially the Internet. For examply, we can see everywhere that people watch short videos on after another for a long time, click on their social software every few minutes. Once they can't ger online , they don't know what to do and may feel anxious , panicked(惊慌失措的) and impatient about everything.

In my opinion , there are two reasons for this phenomenon. One the one hand , people are under great pressure , while the Internet provides a platform for people to relax themselves. By playing mini-games, vocal (有声的) chatting with friends , watching movies and listening to music, people can easily forget their problems temporary. On the other hand , technology developers tend to create addictive products to attract more users. Leading to excessive (过分的) digital consumption among young people, and making them increasingly dependent on their phones.

Therefore, people should take technology addiction seriously. Turn off our phnoes, go outside and communicate with others face to face. Do more exercise and form optimistic attitude towards life!

Is technology making people lazy ?

2022-11-10

With the continuous progress of science and technology,great changes have taken place in the way people live. This progress has brought great convenience to people. Nonetheless , it has also made many people lazy.

On the one hand , prviding ready-made food straight to the doorstep , food delivery service is booming now, and the younger generation is not willing to cook by themselves , or even visit a restaurant. On the other hand , online shopping is more enjoyable for people. Many people forgo the tiring activity of going to the small , instead , they lie in the sofa and just move their fingers. Therefore , the decline in exercise has left many people in decling health.

There is no denying that technology enjoys many advantages. For example, the Internet enables people to know the latest news all over the world. However , it also makes people lazy. Consequently, while improving the quality of life, people should learn to use science and technology correctly instead of relying on it blindly.
